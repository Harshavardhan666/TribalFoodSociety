Tribal food Soceity

About our Project

The proposed project aims to address multiple facets of sustainable development by focusing on the preservation of traditional food crops and agricultural practices, with a special emphasis on involving tribal communities. By integrating Information and Communication Technology (ICT), the initiative seeks to market indigenous tribal food recipes, thereby contributing to the economic empowerment of these communities. The project envisions the inclusion of disadvantaged youth in various aspects, such as promoting, marketing, and selling these unique traditional food products. To enhance visibility and accessibility, the plan involves establishing permanent food stalls in strategic locations such as tourist resorts, forest guest houses, and other key tourist destinations. This holistic approach not only supports the conservation of cultural heritage but also promotes sustainable economic development for the Particularly Vulnerable Tribal Groups.
